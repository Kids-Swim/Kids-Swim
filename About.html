Kids Swim University

Kids Swim University provides electronic tools and resources to help promote water safety and swim instruction in the community.

We find that organizations who own and operate pools do a fine job providing physical resources (equipment, instructors, etc.) but don't often dedicate the time, energy, and focus toward developing a library accessible to the public. The various instruction manuals and worksheets they have developed are stuck on VHS and, at best, DVDs that they only make available to those who register for and attend a 30- to 40-hour training course. So we are creating a website to make more resources available and bridge that gap. The main objective there is to help parents who already understand the value of swim lessons and might even already have kids enrolled in lessons get the most out of the time and money they are investing by providing guidance for their between-lesson time.
